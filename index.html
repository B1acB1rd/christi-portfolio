About Me

My name is Adewole Christianah Omowunmi.

I am a freelance runway and fashion model based in Lagos, Ibadan, and Abeokuta. With a strong commitment to excellence, I have walked for several reputable fashion brands and high-profile events, including Lush Hair and Bare Fashion Evening (The Runway Heist), among others. I also specialize in fashion campaigns and creative visual projects.

Beyond modeling, I am a civil engineering student, a leader within my academic community, and a creative who enjoys photography, content creation, and collaborating with fashion-forward brands.

Thank you for visiting my space. I look forward to creating something beautiful with you.
